I’m Lil Traumatized, and I have a deep appreciation and love for visual art. As an art appreciator, I’m drawn to striking, unique visual styles, particularly in animation and illustration.

In 2025, I found new inspiration to create more art myself, and I’ve been working across photography, videography, and 2D animation. I’ve also started DJing for myself.

Quick facts:

  • Based out of Los Angeles.
  • My biggest hobby is friendship.
  • In terms of technical art ability I am most proficient in photography.
  • I love music and attend festivals and concerts whenever I can.

My choice of artist name comes from a love of rap music and the various musical artists that prepend their names with Lil. In conjunction, I also like the play on words that not only am I lil traumatized, I myself am a little traumatized. Being a human on this planet, you yourself are also a little traumatized. We are all a lil traumatized. No one makes it out of this life unscathed.
